INGREDIENTS
2 BREAD SLICES
1 LETTUCE
4 CUCUMBER
1 CHEESE SLICE
4 TOMATO SLICES

STEPS:
1. Put 2 bread slices side by side
2. Put lettuce on two slices of bread
3. Put 2 tomato slices on each slice of bread
4. Put cheese slice on one of the slice of bread
5. Put 2 cucumber on each slice of bread
6. Put 1 slice on top of the other
7. VOILA!
